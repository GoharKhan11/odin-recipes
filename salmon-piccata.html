Salmon Piccata

Ingredients

  • 3 ounce skinless salmon fillet
  • salt and ground black pepper (as desired)
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup dry white wine
  • 1 tablespoon finely minced garlic
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon drained capers
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1/2 sliced lemon

Steps

  1. Season salmon fillets with salt and pepper.
  2. Pour flour in a bowl and dredge the salmon in the flour. Remove excess flour.
  3. Heat oil on medium heat. Cook salmon fillets in oil until brown on both sides, roughly 3 minutes of cooking on each side.
  4. Temporarily store fillet and keep warm.
  5. Pour wine into skillet, scrape away brown bits from the bottom of the skillet.
  6. Cook garlic in skillet till fragrant and brown and only half liquid is left (about 1 minute).
  7. Add chicken broth, lemon juice and capers to skillet. Bring to boil while stirring.
  8. Add butter to skillet, stir till melted.
  9. Return salmon fillets to skillet, spoon sauce over them.
  10. Cook, turning fillets once, until salmon is cooked through and flakes easily with a fork (about 4 minutes).
  11. Serve salmon with sauce poured over it and garnish with lemon slices.
