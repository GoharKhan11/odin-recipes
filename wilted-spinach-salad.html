Wilted Spinach Salad

Ingredients

  • 8 eggs
  • 1 pound bacon
  • 2 bunches of fresh spinach (rined and dried)
  • 4 thinly sliced green onions
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 cup white vinegar
  • 1/4 cup red wine vinegar

Steps

  1. Place 6 eggs in medium saucepan, submerge in cold water.
  2. Bring water to boild and immediately remove from heat.
  3. Cover saucepan and let eggs sit in hot water for 10-12 minutes. Remove eggs, cool, peel and chop.
  4. Cook bacon in large deep skillet on medium heat till brown. Drain, crumb and set aside the bacon, reserving 1/2 cup of drippings in the skillet.
  5. Toss together spinach and green onions in a bowl.
  6. Heat reserved drippings on low heat
  7. Whisk together remaining 2 eggs, sugar, white vinegar and red win vinegar.
  8. Add whisked mix to warm greese, whisk for about a minute till thickened.
  9. Immediately pour whisked mixture over spinach, add bacon and toss to coat.
  10. Garnish with chopped egg.
