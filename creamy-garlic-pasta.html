Creamy Garlic Pasta

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • 4 minced cloves of garlic
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 3 cups chicken broth (more as needed)
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 pound spaghetti
  • 1 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 3/4 cup heavy cream
  • 1 and 1/2 tablespoons dried parsley

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in medium pan over medium heat.
  2. Add garlic to pan, stir till fragrant (1-2 minutes).
  3. Add butter to pan, stir constantly till melted.
  4. Pour 3 cups chicken broth, add salt and pepper, bring to boil.
  5. Add spaghetti to pan, cook, stirring occassionally till tender yet firm (about 12 minutes).
  6. Add more chicken broth if pasta starts sticking to the pan.
  7. Add Parmesan cheese, cream, and parsley and mix until thoroughly combined. Serve immediately.
